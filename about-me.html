About Me

Twinkle, twinkle, little star, How I wonder what you are!

On clear nights, when the sky stretches endlessly above, I can’t help but step outside. Under the quiet presence of Orion and Cassiopeia, I find myself wondering about the vastness of it all. Is there a hidden pattern out there, a piece of the cosmic puzzle we haven’t discovered yet? This curiosity is what drives me. I’m fascinated by the universe—its nebulae, black holes, and the mysteries that lie beyond. Questions about wormholes and parallel universes aren’t just science fiction to me; they’re possibilities waiting to be explored.
